Abstract

We propose SkeletonDiffusion, a novel nonisotropic latent diffusion model outperforming conventional isotropic diffusion with fewer parameters and faster convergence. We preserve the notion of body joint in every layer of our network and design our architecture to explicitly exploit the skeleton graph structure, amplifying the impact of our nonisotropic formulation on the hierarchical latent variables. Our model achieves state-of-the-art performance on real-world benchmarks Human3.6M and AMASS by generating realistic and diverse motions.
